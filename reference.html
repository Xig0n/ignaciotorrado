Selection

Everything has been invented, they say. And what works, trite. In this scenario, there are two ways to differentiate: follow the rules or invent them. The difficult thing about inventing new rules is that success is not assured. And first, you have to break a few. That's why I try to draw inspiration from particularly daring works.
